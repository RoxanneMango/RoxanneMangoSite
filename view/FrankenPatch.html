FrankenPatch

FrankenPatch is a top-down RPG C++ game originally created for the 2022 Cozy Autumn Game Jam on itch.io. The game engine is made in conjunction with SFML for I/O handling and rendering.
Play as a character that gets transported during a thunder storm to a pumpkin field in a doctor's backyard where you are assailed by ... zombies?! Uncover the town mystery and find your way home, if you can!
